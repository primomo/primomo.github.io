Calling all Queen fans, this is Primomo's offering to find the best ever song by this incredible band. When Freddie mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor and John Deacon formed in London in 1970 little did they know that they will go on to be one of the biggest selling, most electrifying rock bands of all time. Freddie Mercury was regarded as one of the greatest rock frontmen of all time, thier 1985 Live Aid performance at Wembley Stadium regularly tops many industry polls as one of the greatest shows in rock history. Queen have amassed an incredible 18 number one albums, with record sales in the hundreds of millions.
The tracks featured in this list include Don't stop me now, We will rock you and Killer Queen. As well as the massive hits, Bohemian Rhapsody, this single was released in 1970 and featured on the album and night at the opera. Bohemian Rhapsody is the third biggest selling single in the UK of all time. It reached number one in the UK twice and returns charts again peaking at number two in 1992 after its appearance in hit film Wayne's world. In 2012, TV network ITV ran a poll to find the Nations favourite number one, Bohemian Rhapsody topped this nationwide poll and will do very well in our league but is it your top Queen song?
