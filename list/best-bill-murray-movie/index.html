We are huge fans of Bill Murray here at the Primomo, he is high on our list of people we would most like to have a beer with. After making his big breakthrough on US TV show Saturday Night Live, Bill Murray has gone on to steal scenes in numerous successful movies. He famously does not have an agent, which probably explains the mixup involving the film Garfield, he allegedly agreed to do it because he thought it was a Coen brothers film, it wasn't. Garfield: The movie does not appear on our shortlist, only his finest work does.
The list for Best Bill Murray Movie includes classic performances such as his betrayal of Dr Peter Venkman in the 1984 film Ghostbusters & his star turn as Phil Connors in Groundhog Day. Another film we expect to rank high on this list is Caddyshack, a movie with he filmed in just six days and improvised every single line, he is such a cool dude! We are also huge fans of Wes Anderson, if you are too, check out the Best Wes Anderson Movie game in the related list section, Bill Murray has appeared in pretty much all of Anderson's movies. The Grand Budapest Hotel, Rushmore and The Life Aquatic are all on this shortlist. Of course no best of Bill Murray list is complete without Lost in Translation which earned Murray an Oscar nomination. We think all these films are pretty brilliant, but can you decide which is your favourite Bill Murray film?
