The James Bond films conjure up many images, the gadgets, the car, the women and of course the action. But another crucial ingredient we demand from our favourite secret agent is the iconic silhouetted opening sequence. This is where the inspiration for this game came from as we ask you to pick your favourite James Bond theme song.
Being selected to sing the theme tune to a James Bond film has always been an honour to any recording artist. Shirley Bassey became synonymous with James Bond theme tunes having recorded the main track on three separate occasions, Goldfinger, Diamonds are Forever and Moonraker. Adele scored the movie franchise's first ever Oscar for her recording of the title track for Skyfall. Tom Jones with Thunderball, Duran Duran with A View for a Kill and Carly Simon's iconic Nobody Does it Better from The Spy Who Loved Me will all rank high on this game. Have your say and pick your favourite song, don't forget to check out other James Bond lists within the site.
