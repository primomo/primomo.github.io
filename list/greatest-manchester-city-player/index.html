Formed in 1880 as Saint Marks and taking their current name in 1894, Manchester City football club are a major force within the English Premier League. Having moved from their Maine Road home to their current City of Manchester Stadium in 2003, they have enjoyed recent major success. In 2008 they were bought by the Abu Dhabi United group, as a result Forbes magazine has place them within the top 10 most valuable football clubs in the world. With this great wealth has come great success with the Premier League titles in 2012 and 2014 as well as the various domestic cup successes. Some of the finest Premier League talent has been attracted to this Manchester club, some of which are featured on this list.

The clubs are the most successful period was during the late 1960s and early 1970s. The club won the league championship, FA Cup, league cup and also enjoyed cup victory in Europe in the European cup winners cup. The players from this successful team became Manchester City legends and were idolised by the city faithful.
