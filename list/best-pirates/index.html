It seems to us here at Primomo Towers that the world has gone completely mad for pirates. And admittedly, we can see why due to their restless spirit, the command their ships and and their Lawless adventures on the high seas. But who is the greatest Pirates of them all?

Pirate come in many forms it could be a guy with one leg and a patch over his eye with parrot on the shoulder or a cool dude with long hair that looks like a rockstar (see Keith Richards), actually, I guess they all do look the same.

So who is it going to be your favourite pirate? Will you go for old hook hand Captain Hook from Peter Pan or how about loveable oddball Steve the Pirate from Dodgeball? Fancy a drink, yes please, how about Captain Morgan possibly the tastiest of the Pirates. Would you prefer to climb aboard the Black Pig and sell the seven seas with Captain Pugwash? Of course pirates were real, William Kidd, Bartholomew Roberts or lady Pirate Anne Bonny make the shortlist. Or will your favourite pirate be the swashbuckling Captain Jack Sparrow from the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise, he's about to set sail on his fifth Big budget adventure.
