This list is to celebrate the cinematic career of McCauley Culkin. Play this game and give your opinion of the best McCauley Culkin Movie Ever, using your votes and the many other Primomo players we will be able to ascertain his all time greatest film.
McCauley Culkin became world wide superstar in the late 80s and early 90s, in a four year period he appeared in four hugely successful movies. In 1989 he starred in the John Hughes picture Uncle Buck alongside John candy, this family comedy went on to gross nearly 80 million dollars at the box office. This sum would be dwarfed the following year by another John Hughes written film,  Home Alone, which became the highest grossing live action comedy of all time. The McCauley Culkin juggernaut kept on rolling with successful turns in My Girl and Home Alone 2: Lost in New York.
We would expect these early films to be up near the top of our league table but maybe you preferred  Getting Even with Dad, Richie Rich or his return to the big screen after nine years away, Space Monster?
