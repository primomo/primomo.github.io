The 11th August 2014 will remain a very sad day in the world of entertainment as one of its leading lights sadly passed away. Robin Williams started his entertainment career in the mid-1970s as a stand up comedian in San Francisco and Los Angeles, a short stint on television and then a near 30 years on the big screen followed. This list is to celebrate the life in film of Robin Williams.
Robin Williams rose to fame on the hit TV sitcom Mork and Mindy which was a spin-off from one of the highest watched shows of the time, Happy Days. Williams became a household name as audiences loved his quirky improvised comedy style. Hollywood beckoned by 1980 as he took his first starring role in the film Popeye, this was the start of his film career which would last until his sad death in 2014.
This list includes all of Robin Williams's greatest films from zany kids adventures to sensitive moving dramas, the acting range of Williams was superb. The shortlist here highlights this spectacular range - Hook, Jumanji and Mrs Doubtfire sit together with films such as Dead Poets Society, Good Morning, Vietnam and One Hour Photo. Robin Williams received many accolades for his acting including an Oscar in the Best Supporting Actor category for the 1997 picture Good Will Hunting, written by Matt Damon and Ben Affleck.
